About ForgeUrFit

We help people create clothing that represents them.

ForgeUrFit is a custom clothing business focused on personalized streetwear-style clothing for teens and young adults. Our main products are hoodies, shirts, and sweatpants.

Customers can choose our own brand designs or request custom designs based on their personal ideas. Each item is made to feel personal, whether it is for a school club, sports team, friend group, event, gift, or everyday outfit.

Name meaning

Forge means to create or build. UrFit means your outfit and personal style. Together, ForgeUrFit means customers can build clothing that feels like their own.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to help people express themselves through affordable, stylish, and high-quality custom clothing.

By focusing on customization, fair prices, and local connections in the GTA, we make fashion more personal and meaningful for teens and young adults.

Customization
